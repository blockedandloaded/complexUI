Tokenizing Your Guns into PISTLs

blocked + loaded ties each gun to anon-fungible ERC721 token called a PISTL that is uniquely yours.

Utilizing Smart Contract Technology

If you choose to sell a gun to another user, the transaction occurs by means of a smart contract and the token is traded.

Proving Ownership Permanently

Transactions are then stores on the blockchain, making them permanent and immutable. This is where proof of past and present ownership exists.

Building Onto the Ethereum Network

The network is built on the ethereum network; ether is necessary to fuel transactions, which include purchasing and selling PISTLs.
